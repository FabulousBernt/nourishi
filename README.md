# The Plateful

AI-powered recipe discovery and meal planning app.

Available at **[theplateful.app](https://theplateful.app)**

## Description

Finding what to cook shouldn't be a chore. Existing recipe sites are cluttered with intrusive ads, long personal stories, and hard-to-navigate interfaces. The Plateful was built to give you a clean, fast way to discover recipes — whether you know exactly what you want, are working with whatever is in your fridge, or need a structured meal plan for the week ahead. We serve minimal, non-intrusive ads to keep the site free and running, without ruining your experience.

## Quick Start

Open **[theplateful.app](https://theplateful.app)** in any browser. No account or sign-up needed. Start searching for recipes immediately or tap the pantry tab to add ingredients you have on hand.

## Usage

- **Recipe Search** — Type what you're craving in natural language (e.g. "creamy pasta with chicken" or "something spicy"). Results combine AI-generated recipes and web-sourced recipes from TheMealDB. Use the toggles to filter by source.
- **Pantry Mode** — List ingredients you have available. The app finds recipes that use those ingredients, reducing food waste and trips to the store.
- **Meal Planner** — Choose a goal (bulking, cutting, low-carb, vegetarian protein, balanced, Mediterranean, family-friendly, or quick meals) and generate a full 1-week meal plan with breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.
- **Nutrition & Macros** — Every recipe and meal plan displays calories, protein, carbs, and fat with visual macro bars.
- **Export** — Download meal plans as printable PDFs or import them into your calendar app via calendar export.
- **Rating** — Star-rate any recipe to help the community find the best dishes.

Your feedback directly shapes what gets built next.

## Tech Stack

- **React 19 / Next.js** — UI framework
- **Cerebras AI** — AI-powered recipe generation and nutrition estimation
- **TheMealDB** — Web recipe database
- **Neon (Postgres)** — Community-curated recipe database with star ratings
- **Vercel** — Hosting & serverless functions
- **Capacitor 8** — Native iOS/Android compilation
- **Vitest** — Testing
